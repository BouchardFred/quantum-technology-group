Positions

Join the Quantum Technology Group

We welcome motivated postdoctoral researchers, graduate students, undergraduate students, and visitors interested in hands-on quantum photonics and ultrafast optics.

What we look for

Useful backgrounds include quantum optics, nonlinear optics, ultrafast photonics, experimental physics, electronics, optical design, programming, and theory or simulation.

Curiosity, care in the lab, and collaborative habits matter as much as prior specialization.

How to apply

Email a short note with your CV, research interests, and the type of position you are seeking.
